How it works

Three steps, one week, no paperwork to dig up.

  1. 01

    You apply

    The application is ten short sections and takes about ten minutes. No pay stubs, tax returns, or benefits letters.

  2. 02

    We review it privately

    Applications go to the club directors. They aren't shared with coaches or other families, and we don't announce who receives an award.

  3. 03

    You hear back in a week

    We email you our decision within one week. If we can't cover everything, we'll tell you what we can cover.

What we ask in return

A scholarship isn't a handout. It's the club investing in your athlete, and we ask your family to invest back.

From your athlete

  • ★ Attend practice consistently
  • ★ Let the coaches know about absences
  • ★ Show respect to coaches and teammates
  • ★ Demonstrate good sportsmanship
  • ★ Give maximum effort

From your family

Help the club where you can. You'll pick what fits your schedule right on the application.

  • ★ Tournament setup
  • ★ Fundraising
  • ★ Cleaning the facility
  • ★ Social media or photography
  • ★ Wherever you're needed

Scholarship FAQ

The questions families ask us before they apply.

How much does a scholarship cover?

Awards are based on need and can cover up to your full monthly club dues and/or your athlete's USA Wrestling membership. Some families need part of their dues covered, some need all of it, and some only need help with the USA Wrestling membership. Tell us what your family needs and we'll work from there.

Do I have to send pay stubs or tax returns?

No. There's no income documentation required. The application asks about your family's situation in your own words, and that's enough for us to start the conversation.

Who sees my application?

Applications are confidential and reviewed by the club directors. They aren't shared with coaches or other families, and we don't announce who is on scholarship.

How long will it take to hear back?

We respond within one week of receiving your application. If we can't cover everything you asked for, we'll tell you what we can cover.

How long does an award last?

Awards run for the season and are reviewed periodically as funding and family circumstances change. If your situation changes before then, call or text the club and we'll work it out.

Can it help with camps, clinics, or gear?

Possibly. Dues and USA Wrestling membership come first, but other opportunities such as camps, clinics, tournament entries, and gear can be considered by request. Ask on the application.

What if funding runs out?

Awards depend on the funds available when we review an application, so we can't say yes to everyone. If we can't help right away we'll tell you, you're welcome to apply again, and free Sunday practice stays open to your athlete either way.

My child has never wrestled. Should we still apply?

Yes. Start with free Sunday CSRA Practice or your first 3 free classes so your athlete can try the sport, then apply whenever you're ready. You don't need experience to apply.
