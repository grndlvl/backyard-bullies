Our Mission

Since opening in 2023, Backyard Bullies Wrestling Club has focused on creating a positive, competitive environment where wrestlers develop their skills, build confidence, and become leaders both on and off the mat.

We believe wrestling changes lives through:

  • Discipline
  • Accountability
  • Hard Work
  • Respect
  • Perseverance
  • Leadership

About Backyard Bullies

Backyard Bullies Wrestling Club welcomes athletes from all backgrounds and experience levels. Whether you're stepping on the mat for the first time or chasing a state title, we have a place for you.

  • › Wrestling for the first time
  • › Preparing for your school season
  • › Looking to compete at a higher level
  • › Training for state or national competition

Founded in 2023, Backyard Bullies has quickly become a home for wrestlers throughout the CSRA who want high-level instruction, challenging training, and a supportive team culture.
