Coach Justin Boyden

Founder & Head Coach

Justin Boyden is the Founder and Head Coach of Backyard Bullies Wrestling Club, a wrestling organization dedicated to developing athletes through discipline, hard work, and community involvement.

Originally from Beckley, West Virginia, Coach Boyden served more than 20 years in the U.S. military, including multiple combat deployments. Throughout his military career, wrestling remained a constant passion and source of personal growth. As a competitive athlete, he earned recognition as a two-time All-Army Wrestler and developed a coaching philosophy centered on toughness, accountability, and resilience.

Coach Boyden is committed to creating opportunities for all wrestlers regardless of experience level. He has been instrumental in expanding girls wrestling opportunities, organizing free community practices, hosting camps and clinics, and helping athletes pursue their goals on the national stage. His dedication to athlete development has also earned him opportunities to serve as a coach with Team Georgia Wrestling's national team staff.

Beyond wins and losses, Coach Boyden believes wrestling is a vehicle for building character, confidence, and leadership.

Coach Hallee

Head Strength Coach

CrossFit Level 1 coach helping athletes build strength safely, develop confidence, and create habits that support discipline, resilience, and mental toughness.

Bio & highlights +

Coaching Philosophy

Mental toughness, consistency, and embracing hard things build strength for life.

Credentials & Highlights

  • Head Strength Coach, Backyard Bullies Wrestling Club
  • CrossFit Level 1 Certified
  • 5+ Years Training, Competing, and Lifting
  • Endurance Race & Fitness Competition Experience
  • Strength, Confidence, and Resilience Development
  • Homeschool Mom of Three Boys

Hallee is the Head Strength Coach for Backyard Bullies Wrestling Club. She is a homeschool mom of three boys, and fitness has become a huge part of her life.

She earned her CrossFit Level 1 certification and has been training, competing, and lifting for over five years. During that time, she developed a passion not only for fitness, but for helping others discover what they are capable of.

One of the biggest lessons she hopes to pass on to young athletes is that they can do hard things. Whether that is lifting a challenging weight, pushing through a tough workout, competing in a wrestling match, staying disciplined with school, helping at home, or choosing to do the right thing when it is difficult, growth happens when athletes embrace challenges.

As Head Strength Coach, her goal is to help athletes build strength safely, develop confidence, and create habits that will benefit them both on and off the mat.

Coach Katie Huitron

Coach

Lifelong combat sports athlete and coach helping wrestlers build confidence, discipline, resilience, and self-belief through hard work and a positive mindset.

Bio & highlights +

Coaching Philosophy

Success starts with believing in yourself, working hard, staying focused, and never giving up.

Credentials & Highlights

  • Mixed Martial Arts Since Age Seven
  • Sparring & Grappling Tournament Experience
  • Beach Wrestling Coaching Experience
  • World Championships Coaching Travel to Greece
  • Active MMA, Kickboxing/Muay Thai, and Wrestling Training
  • Faglier's MMA Family Gym

Katie Huitron has been involved in mixed martial arts since the age of seven, developing a lifelong passion for combat sports and athletic competition. As a youth athlete, she competed in numerous sparring and grappling tournaments, building the foundation of discipline, perseverance, and determination that she now brings to coaching and mentoring.

Her journey into wrestling began four years ago when her oldest daughter, Esperanza, stepped onto the mat. Before long, her three younger children followed, turning wrestling into a true family passion. Wanting to support their growth and the growth of other young athletes, Katie began helping coach beach wrestling and quickly discovered a love for mentoring and developing wrestlers of all ages.

One of the proudest moments of her coaching career was traveling to Greece for the World Championships with Esperanza and Josiah, where both athletes earned 7th-place finishes against some of the best competitors in the world.

Today, Katie continues to actively train in MMA, kickboxing/Muay Thai, and wrestling, leading by example and demonstrating that learning and growth never stop. As a proud member of her family's gym, Faglier's MMA, and a coach with Backyard Bullies Wrestling Club, she is committed to creating a supportive environment where athletes are challenged to reach their full potential.

Katie believes that success starts with believing in yourself. Her message to every athlete is simple: if you are willing to work hard, stay focused, and never give up, you can accomplish anything you set your mind to. Philippians 4:13.

Coach Dale Schmacht

Assistant Coach

Former Illinois state champion and three-time NCAA Division III All-American who teaches hard work, self-discipline, goal setting, and high-level preparation.

Bio & highlights +

Coaching Philosophy

Hard work, self-discipline, and achieving established goals: the mindset he teaches every wrestler.

Credentials & Highlights

  • 1979 Illinois State Champion
  • Three-Time NCAA Division III All-American
  • Three-Time Hall of Fame Inductee
  • MD & PhD in Physiology
  • Academic Trauma & Critical Care Surgeon
  • Head Wrestling Coach, Seigler's (since 2008)
  • Assistant Coach, Backyard Bullies (since 2024)
  • Mentor to CSRA State Champions & Place-Winners

Coach Dale Schmacht was born in Rock Island, Illinois, and graduated from Cambridge High School in Cambridge, Illinois. As a high school wrestler, he was the 1978 Illinois Class A state runner-up at 138 pounds and the 1979 Illinois Class A state champion at 145 pounds.

Coach Schmacht continued his wrestling career at Augustana College, where he became a three-time NCAA Division III All-American. After college, he earned both a PhD in Physiology and a Medical Degree before building a career in academic medicine and surgery.

Coach Schmacht has remained active in local coaching for many years, including Head Wrestling Coach at Seigler's Karate Center since 2008 and Assistant Coach at Backyard Bullies Wrestling Club since 2024.

Hard work, self-discipline, and achieving established goals have shaped Coach Schmacht's path in life, and he teaches with that same mindset.
